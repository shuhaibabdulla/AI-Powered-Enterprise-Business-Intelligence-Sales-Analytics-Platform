# Enterprise AI Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform

## Overview

Enterprise AI Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform is an end-to-end business analytics solution designed to transform raw business data into meaningful insights using Business Intelligence, Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Interactive Dashboards.

The platform demonstrates a complete analytics workflow including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, KPI reporting, sales forecasting, customer analytics, executive dashboards, automated reporting, and AI-powered business recommendations.

This project is designed as a portfolio-ready enterprise analytics solution suitable for Business Intelligence, Data Analytics, and AI-focused roles.
